In my previous life, I was neglected and lived all of my days on a chain otuside. However, I was found my some kind people from the Rescue Project who saved me, and helped me find my forever family. Can you wine and dine me like I deserve? Better yet, bring squeaky toys!

  • Eyes: green

Nice to meet you, my name is Theodora, and I'm the coolest cat you've ever met. I'm a strong and independent spirit with an active social life outside of my home. I love to go outside and visit the other families in my neighborhood (I may or may not be searching for a new family). I was hit by a car this summer while out doing cat stuff, and shattered my pelvis. It cost my parent's a few thousand dollars to put me back together - and it was a month before their wedding! I guess it's a testament to how lovable I am. They tried to keep me inside after that, but I screamed constantly and uprooted the house plants until they relented and let me out again (after a successful recovery, of course). Like I said, I'm very spirited. Now that I have a new lease on life, I'm looking for love. I'm not sure how this will work out, since I tend to dislike most other creatures, but I'm willing to give it a try. Small dogs need not apply, I wouldn't be able to resist the urge to scratch your face.
